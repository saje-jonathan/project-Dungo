Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply go to the 'Coffees' or 'Teas' page, enter the quantity for your desired drinks, and click the "Place My Order" button at the bottom.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, our digital manager supports Cash on Delivery and E-Wallet payments via the confirmation modal.

Can I customize my drink?

Our current version provides standard recipes. Future updates will include options for milk alternatives and extra espresso shots!
